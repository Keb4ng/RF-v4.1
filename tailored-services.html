FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We hold a regular weekly call with you to keep you informed on the hours spent and the hours remaining. It will give you a realistic outcome on what can be achieved within the month itself.

We have a well-managed Jira board that will be shared with you (if you have your own, we can use that too!) and we document everything we do (e.g., requirements, solution design, process diagrams, handovers, etc.).

We are a proud Salesforce partner and we love the Salesforce platform. However, our expertise extends further than that. For example, we have experts in WSO2 and AWS.

You are only limited by the hours you have purchased per month.

You can purchase additional hours at our day rate, or, alternatively, wait until the next month starts, depending on your needs.

There may be occasions whereby you want to bundle some hours together. This can be agreed with our PM on a case-by-case basis.
